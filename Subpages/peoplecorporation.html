People Corporation

Introduction

People Corporation is a Desk Simulation game from the perspective of an (evil) corporation. The goal of this game is to give the player insight on how these companies operate, manipulate the masses and pollute the environment.
In this game, the player takes on the roll of a nameless employee whose reward system is directly linked to the largest polluting corporation in the world, People Corporation.
De player plays through a standard workweek where they have to accept or deny different documents provided by the company. The choices made will influence the world around the player, which theyâ€™ll be confronted with by the end of each workday through reading the news.
In doing so we shed light on the impact existing companies have on the real life world.

About the project

In this project I took on the role as the developer, in charge of all the programming in the project.
During this time I've created the diffirent mechanics, like drag/drop, stamps and other interactables, as well as the underlying systems, like the statistics, news network and alternative endings.
