CQuencer

Introduction

CQuencer is an audio sequence tool for designers to help them create small chiptune audio fragments or sound effects. While researching existing audio sequence tools I noticed that most of them are aimed at experienced musicians, including (over)complicated and hard to navigate interfaces for inexperienced designers.
In my design I aim at those inexperienced designers and try to create a simple audio sequence tool designers can use to create simple audio fragments and music needed in their projects.

Demo

What makes this tool special is that it uses no preloaded audiofiles to create the tones. CQuencer uses procedurally generated audio clips.
